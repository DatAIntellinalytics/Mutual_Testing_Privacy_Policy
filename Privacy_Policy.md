## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Mutual Testing** ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application **Mutual Testing** (the "App").

Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** When you register or sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by your Google account.
- **App Submissions:** When you submit an app for testing, we collect the app name, Play Store URL, app icon, and any description you provide.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Statistics:** With your explicit permission, we use Android's **Usage Access** (Package Usage Stats) permission to verify that you have actually opened and used the test apps you agreed to test. This data is used solely to validate testing activity and award coins accordingly. This data is processed on-device and only the verification result (time spent) is sent to our servers.
- **Device Information:** We may collect basic device information such as device model, Android version, and app version for debugging and support purposes.

### 2.3 Permissions Required
The App requests the following Android permissions, each for a specific purpose:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `INTERNET` | Communicating with our backend (Supabase) to sync data |
| `PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS` | Verifying that test apps are genuinely used |
| `SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW` | Showing a floating testing-timer overlay while you test apps |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Sending reminders about daily testing goals |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Running the testing timer reliably in the background |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Rescheduling notification alarms after device restart |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` / `USE_EXACT_ALARM` | Delivering notifications at precise times |
| `VIBRATE` | Vibration feedback for notifications |
| `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | Checking network connectivity before syncing data |

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following cases:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Supabase** (supabase.com): Our backend infrastructure provider for database, authentication, and file storage. Data is stored securely on Supabase servers. See [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- **Google Sign-In** (Google LLC): Used for authentication. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Google AdMob** (Google LLC): Used to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalisation. See [Google's Privacy Policy for Advertising](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (via **Profile → Settings → Delete Account**), all your personal data—including your profile, app submissions, testing history, and coins—is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.
